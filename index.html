Find out about some effective charities

  • Giving What We Can - an organisation that both makes charity recommentations and aims to encourage people to give 10% of their income to what it considers the world's most effective charities.
  • GiveWell - a charity evaluator.

Find out more about effective altruism and related ideas

  • Doing Good Better - a book about how to know you're making a difference by Will MacAskill, Associate Professor of Philosohpy at Oxford University and co-founder of Giving What We Can.
  • Effective Altruism TedX Talk - an oberview of effective altruism by Beth Barnes.
  • 80,000 Hours - an organisation applying this same thinking to how you should spend the 80,000 hours of your career most effectively to help the world.
  • The Centre for Effective Altruism - Oxford-based researchers and umbrella organisation of both Giving What We Can and 80,000 Hours.
